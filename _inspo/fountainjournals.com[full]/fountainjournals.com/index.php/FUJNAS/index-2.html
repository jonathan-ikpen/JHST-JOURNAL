About the Journal

MISSION STATEMENT

Fountain Journal of Natural and Applied Sciences (FUJNAS) is the official publication of the College of Natural and Applied Sciences, Fountain University, Osogbo, Nigeria. The journal seeks to:

  1. serve scientists by publishing significant advances in the fields of natural and applied sciences; and
  2. ensure prompt and rapid dissemination of scientific information to the world for the purpose of scientific knowledge/education, lifestyle, culture and career development
