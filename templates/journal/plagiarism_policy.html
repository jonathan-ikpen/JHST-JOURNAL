Plagiarism

Each manuscript will be subjected to a plagiarism check before being sent for double-blind peer review. Manuscripts will only be processed for peer review if the similarity index is not more than ten (10) percent.

This Journal uses Plagiarism CheckerX (or equivalent industry standard software) for its similarity index check.
