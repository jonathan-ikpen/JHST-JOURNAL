An Introduction to the Journal

From the Chief Editor’s Desk

Welcome to the official page of the Journal of Hydrocarbon Science & Technology (JHST), a flagship publication of the Petroleum Training Institute (PTI), Effurun. As the Chief Editor, it is my distinct pleasure to introduce you to our journal, a platform dedicated to the advancement of knowledge and innovation in the dynamic fields of hydrocarbon science and technology.

JHST is committed to disseminating high-quality, peer-reviewed research that addresses the evolving challenges and opportunities within the petroleum and energy sectors. Our scope encompasses a wide range of topics, including exploration, drilling, production, refining, petrochemicals, renewable energy integration, and environmental management. We aim to foster a vibrant academic community by providing a forum for researchers, engineers, policymakers, and industry professionals to share their findings, insights, and perspectives.

Dr. Michael Ardis

Chief Editor, JHST
